This is me!

Hi there!

My name is Victoria Aróstegui and I’m a UX & Product Designer.

After working in business development in the art world and having launched two businesses, I’m now changin my career to what I believe that perfectcly merges my previous experiences: Business, entrepreneurship and creativity.

Design suddenly showed up in my life changing many things I was doing wrong for a very long time. During my MBA and entrepreneurial expereinces, I got to know user centric methodologies. After hearing what UX was for the first time, I started to attend talks and reading about it, I certainly wanted to learn more. Therefore, that’s what took me to the UX/UI bootcamp at Ironhack.

UX design for me, it’s a way to solve problems, undersatnding users’ needs, and relate these to the business values and goals in order to design potencial solutions that matter the most.
